FAQ

Please see below for help with troubleshooting common problems.

Q: Where is my verification email?

Receiving your verification email may take a few minutes. Additionally, please check your Spam/Junk folders if you haven't already.

Q: How do I start creating and favouriting boards?

You need to register for a free account to do so.

Q: Why can't I select or upload certain files?

Please take note of the following restrictions when uploading soundboards:

  • Files must be < 2.5mb in size
  • Only the following image formats are supported: .jpg, .jpeg, and .png
  • Only the following audio formats are supported: .mp3, .wav, .mpga, .aif, .aiff
  • Maximum of 12 sounds per board

Q: Why has my account been banned?

Most likely due to malicious behaviour, such as uploding inapropriate content.

Q: What if I still have questions?

If you still have questions, please write to contact@conchboards.com!
